Viewing Cozmos pages

Use any Web Browser on any Operating system. 

Editing Pages on a Cozmos server

You are most likely going to be editing pages from Firefox.  If that is the case ensure you have the [Firefox extension] that will allow you to do this.

A small amount of highly technical people may be hardcore SeaMonkey users. They may be happy with [hacking seamonkey].

If you really must use Internet Explorer, you may be interested in [editing with microsoft word].

Editing Pages in a Cozmos system using other browsers

Opera (Mac), Safari (Mac), Konqueror (Linux), Galeon (Linux), Camino (Mac) and others changes will need to be changed to have a helper application for editing.  All they need to support is Ctrl-E (or Apple-E) and a preference setting for the name of the executable to launch for editing.

As of early 2007, none of these browsers are compatible.
